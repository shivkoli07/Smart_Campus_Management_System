Bharati Vidyapeeth College of Engineering, Navi Mumbai

Established in 1990, BVCOE is one of the leading engineering colleges affiliated to Mumbai University and approved by AICTE. The college aims for excellence in technical education and holistic student development.Bharti Vidyapeeth College of Engineering is renowned for quality education leading new folds of technical fields. With a progressive mission of “Social Transformation through Dynamic Education”, the institute is consistently striving for the holistic development of the students. We aim at imparting quality education to meet the needs of industry, profession and society; and to achieve excellence in teaching, learning and research.

Campus Overview

Bharati Vidyapeeth College of Engineering (BVCOE) is spread across a modern urban campus well-connected by rail and road, minutes from Kharghar Railway Station and the Sion–Panvel Highway. In a span of twenty five years the College has gained appreciation because of excellent infrastructure, well equipped laboratory facilities, enriched library, qualified faculty and hence the outstanding results of students.
