Frequently Asked Questions

You can file a complaint by selecting the "File Complaint" option from the student complaint dashboard and filling in the required details.

Yes, the system allows anonymous complaints where only the University ID and complaint details are required.

You can track your complaint by entering your roll number in the "Track Complaint" section of the portal.

Complaints can have three statuses:
  • Not Viewed – Complaint not yet reviewed
  • In Progress – Complaint is being addressed
  • Solved – Issue has been resolved

Complaints are reviewed by authorized administrators and concerned department authorities to ensure timely resolution.

Resolution time depends on the nature of the complaint. Most issues are addressed within a few working days.
