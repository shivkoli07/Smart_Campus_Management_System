Smart Campus Management System

Smart Campus Management System is a centralized web-based application designed to automate campus operations such as complaints, attendance, leave management, feedback, campus & academic information access also modern ChatBot Technology , Active Pie Charts makes it more Advanced and Smart .

System Modules

Explore Campus

Displays detailed information about campus infrastructure, academic buildings, laboratories, library, hostels, sports facilities, and student support amenities.
Includes: Campus overview, facilities, and photo gallery.

Departments

Provides department-wise academic information including courses offered, faculty members, HOD details, laboratories, and departmental vision & mission.
Useful for: Students, faculty, and visitors.

Complaint Management

Enables students to register complaints related to academics, hostels, facilities, or administration and track their resolution status online.
Features: Online filing, tracking, and status updates.

Attendance System

Allows faculty or administrators to mark student attendance digitally, while students can view subject-wise attendance percentage anytime.
Benefit: Transparent and paperless attendance tracking.

Leave Application

Students can submit leave applications online for academic or personal reasons, which can be reviewed and approved by faculty or administrators.
Includes: Apply, review, approve/reject workflow.

Feedback System

Collects structured feedback, ratings, and suggestions from students regarding courses, faculty, and campus services to support continuous improvement.
Purpose: Quality enhancement and student satisfaction.
